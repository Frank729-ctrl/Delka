Common Questions

How do I authenticate API requests?

Add the header X-DelkaAI-Key: fd-delka-sk-… to every request. Use your Secret Key for server-side calls.

How do I get a PDF back synchronously vs via webhook?

Omit webhook_url for a synchronous PDF response. Include it to get a job_id and receive the PDF as base64 at your webhook.

What is the rate limit?

Default: 30 requests/minute per key, 60 per IP. Contact admin to increase limits.

Can I use the Publishable Key for CV generation?

No. CV and Cover Letter generation require a Secret Key (sk-). Publishable Keys are for read-only or lightweight endpoints.
