Requirements:
- Tailor it to the Ghanaian job market
- Opening paragraph: strong hook referencing the role
- Middle: 2–3 concrete achievements matching the job
- Closing: confident call to action
- Tone: professional but warm
- Length: 3 short paragraphs, no longer
- Do NOT use generic phrases like "I am writing to express my interest"
